## ðŸ“‹ Warning Qualifications

A team member may receive a warning for any of the following reasons:

### 1. Communication Failures

| Violation | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **No Response for 2 Weeks** | Failing to respond to team messages, manager inquiries, or communication channels for 14 consecutive days without prior notice |
| **Ignoring Direct Requests** | Not acknowledging or responding to direct requests from team leads or managers |
| **Unavailability Without Notice** | Being unreachable without informing the team in advance |

### 2. Task & Story Delivery

| Violation | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Missing Story Without Valid Excuse** | Failing to complete an assigned story/task without providing a valid reason or excuse |
| **Late Deadline Notification** | Not informing the team at least **48 hours before the deadline** if you cannot complete the story |
| **False Commitments** | Promising to complete work when you're not sure you can deliver â€” **only commit to what you're certain you can do** |
| **Repeated Missed Deadlines** | Consistently failing to meet agreed-upon deadlines |

### 3. Professional Conduct

| Violation | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Unprofessional Behavior** | Disrespectful communication, harassment, or inappropriate conduct |
| **Lack of Accountability** | Blaming others, making excuses, or refusing to take responsibility |
| **Not Attending Meetings** | Missing scheduled team meetings without prior notice or valid reason |
| **Poor Work Quality** | Consistently delivering substandard work that requires excessive revisions |

### 4. Team Collaboration

| Violation | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Not Sharing Progress** | Failing to update the team on task progress during stand-ups or check-ins |
| **Blocking Others** | Not completing work that other team members depend on |
| **Refusing to Help** | Not assisting teammates when reasonably requested |

## ðŸ”‘ Key Principles

> **"Don't promise to do something unless you're sure you can do it"**

1. **Be Honest About Capacity** - If you're unsure whether you can complete a task, say so upfront
2. **Communicate Early** - If problems arise, inform the team immediately, not at the deadline
3. **Stay Reachable** - Keep communication channels open and respond in a timely manner
4. **Take Ownership** - Accept responsibility for your commitments and deliverables

When a warning is issued:
1. The member receives an **email notification** with the reason
2. The warning is **logged** in the system
3. For the **3rd warning**, the member is immediately **dropped** from the team
